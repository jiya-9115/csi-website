Cyber for All

Cybersecurity is a right — not a privilege.

Inclusive Cybersecurity Education

“Cyber for All” is Cyber Secured India’s inclusive initiative dedicated to specially-abled individuals. The program ensures equal access to cybersecurity education, online safety, and digital participation.

Our goal is to empower individuals to stay protected online and confidently engage in the digital world without barriers.
